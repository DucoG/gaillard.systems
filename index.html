What I'm Building

Early detection saves lives.

Cancer detected early is often curable. Detected late, it's fatal.

My PhD research at the Netherlands Cancer Institute focused on finding cancer signals in cell-free DNA using deep learning. The patterns are there in the blood, invisible to traditional methods, but detectable with the right models at a fraction of the cost.

I'm building a system to make this accessible: liquid biopsy analysis that could integrate into routine blood work, catching cancers at stage 1 when survival rates are dramatically higher.

Currently developing the prototype. Consulting on AI systems to fund the R&D.
